Agriculture: A Growing Industry

What is Agriculture?

Agriculture is the practice of cultivating soil, growing crops, and raising animals for food, fiber, medicinal plants, and other products used to sustain and enhance human life.

Importance of Agriculture

Agriculture is essential for the survival of humanity. It provides food and raw materials, creates jobs, and supports economies worldwide.

Agriculture also plays a critical role in the development of sustainable practices to ensure the well-being of future generations. Key components include:

  • Soil health
  • Water management
  • Livestock management
  • Innovative farming techniques
